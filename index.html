about me

Hi, I'm Ariel Mordoch. Currently, I'm working at Strategic Technology Consulting (STC) as a model-based systems engineering (MBSE) consultant. Before my time at STC, I served on Boeing's Enterprise MBSE team for 3 years, advising and steering defense and commerical programs throughout the company. My expertise includes requirements management, modeling & sim, digital thread, systems architecture, enterprise architecture, digital thread, tool integration, and more. I am a Cameo tool & API expert and have extensive experience developing custom plugins to improve modeling productivity and integrate external tools. In 2020, I earned my B.S. in BS in Aerospace Engineering from Georiga Tech. I was born in Tel Aviv, Israel and moved to Atlanta, GA when I was 4 years old. I also hold an FAA Private Pilot Certificate -- to which I added an Instrument Airplane rating in 2024 -- and speak English and Hebrew fluently, German at a B1 level, and Spanish at an A2 level.
