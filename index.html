about me

Hi, I'm Ariel Mordoch. I'm a junior at Georgia Tech studying Aerospace Engineering and I recently completed my first co-op rotation at Honda Aircraft Company. I was born in Tel Aviv, Israel and moved to Atlanta, GA when I was 4 years old. I am an active brother of AEPi and a member of the aerospace honor fraternity SGT. I also hold an FAA Private Pilot Certificate and speak English and Hebrew fluently, German at a B1 level, and French at an A1 level.
